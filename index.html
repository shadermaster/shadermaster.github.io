Event and Casual networking

Expand your network by seeing and meeting people and share your moments before, during and after events.

Event networking

Looking for a new business partner? Or just looking for a new buddie? Through SEVN's event networking, you'll find out who's attending the same event as you are. Get the chance to mingle before, during and after it takes place.

Psst! If you can't patricipate, don't worry - we offer virtual participation.

Share moments

Give everyone else a chance to see the event as you see it or see the event from other user's perspective, while sharing moments. With SEVN you'll have eyes everywhere.

If you'll participate virtually, good for you, this is an unique chance for you to see what's happening live in the event.

Casual networking

It's just as easy as it sound! Casual networking enables networking and communication wherever and whenever. See people around you and make sure you won't miss out anyone.
