Privacy Policy

Version 1.0,  May/25/2016

 This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how and when SEVN collects, uses and shares your information when you use the SEVN app.

Definitions
“You”, “Your”, “User” means you as an individual.
“We”, “Us”, “Our” means the SEVN company.
“Personal Information” means information that, individually or together with other data, may be used to identify an individual. This is also referred to as “personally identifiable information”, and includes for example someone’s name or email address.

Information Collection and Use
SEVN does not collect more information than is necessary to provide you with a functioning and effective service.

To use the SEVN app, you need to provide us with certain personal information:
  • your email address or Facebook login, age, and photo for account creation;
  • geolocation data from your device.
Once collected, this information will be processed and stored so that it is possible for us to provide the SEVN service.

To update your SEVN profile and to use the communication facilities within the App we may require access to:
  • your photos so that you can choose a picture from your photo album to upload to the app;
  • your camera so that you can directly upload a picture to the app.

Your profile data and uploaded content is shared with other users of the SEVN App. This data is discoverable within your immediate vicinity (within a 5 km radius) so long as it matches search criteria which you have defined within the App. Users who have purchased a Premium service -which enables them to ‘be virtually at an event/location’ will also see your profile if it there is a search criteria match.

Where one user initiates a communication to another user of the app (for example in the form of a like or a direct message) SEVN transmits this communication. In some instances, such communications will be stored by SEVN until certain criteria are met (for example, users are within the required geographic range, or after both users have liked each other).

Information Sharing and Disclosure
Information may be shared with service providers that are used to help provide the SEVN app:
  • Data storage (for SEVN App generated data) is provided by Amazon Web Services (EC2). Personal data which you upload to the SEVN app will be shared with Amazon. According to SEVN’s contract with Amazon, data is stored on servers located within the EU; 
  • In-app payment is provided for Premium SEVN services. Payment information may therefore be shared with Apple and/or Google.

It may be necessary for SEVN to disclose your information, including personal information when required to do so by law. We may also disclose information if we determine it necessary or appropriate to enforce our terms and conditions, protect our operations or users, or for reasons of national security or other issues of public importance.

Information Protection
SEVN protects your personal information by using technical security measures such as hashed passwords and encryption, both on data in transit and in data stored on servers.

Information Deletion
SEVN deletes your information when it is no longer needed for the purposes of providing the service, or for billing reasons.

If at any point you wish for SEVN to delete information about you, you simply need to contact us info@sevnapp.com.

Your Rights
You can contact SEVN at any time if you want to:
  • request access to information that SEVN has about you, or
  • correct any information SEVN has about you.
If you have any additional questions about SEVN’s collection and storage of data, please contact us info@sevnapp.com.
